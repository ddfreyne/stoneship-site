This is the personal web site of me, Denis Defreyne. I am a software engineer based in Berlin, Germany, and my pronouns are he/him.

In the past, I’ve worked for SoundCloud and BCG Digital Ventures (among others), and I am currently working for Pitch. I’m the creator of Nanoc, a mature static-site generator written in Ruby, and I led the implementation of closecontact, a COVID-19 contact-sharing solution for Berlin clubs.

You might be here because you’re interested in the software I’ve written, the talks I’ve given, the notes I’ve taken (e.g. What’s top of mind), or the software that’s in my toolbox. Or perhaps you want my CV.

The best way to get in touch with me is on Twitter.
